01
NEEV
// Days 1–30 — Foundation

Pehle mahine ka ek hi kaam hai — toot-ti hui cheezein theek karna. Koi bada goal nahi. Sirf ek solid insaan banna jiske upar baaki sab kuch build ho sake.

⚠️ Yaksh, sun: Pehle 30 din koi result nahi dikhega. Log nahi dekhenge. Viral nahi hoga. Aur yahi woh time hai jab 90% log quit kar dete hain. Tu nahi karega.
"You don't rise to the level of your goals. You fall to the level of your systems." — James Clear
WEEK 01 Gandi Aadtein Identify Karo 🔍 ▾
HabitMental

Pehle week mein kuch "karna" nahi hai. Sirf observe karna hai — khud ko judge kiye bina.

1
Audit Day — Kal kya kiya?
Ek kagaz pe likho — subah se raat tak kal ka poora din. Kitna time phone pe gaya? Kya useful tha, kya waste? Seedha likho — jhooth mat bolo khud se.
2
Screen Time Reality Check
Phone settings mein ja aur screen time report dekho. Jo number aaye, usse note karo. Instagram scroll = converted time to money — kitna gaya?
3
Energy Drain Map
3 cheezein likho jo tujhe sabse zyada energy drain karti hain — chahe woh log hoon, apps hoon ya thoughts. Ek temporary solution soch.
4–5
Sleep Pattern Fix Start
Aaj se raat 11 baje phone band. Seriously. Alarm lagao 10:45 ka. Pehle 2 din mushkil lagega — normal hai.
6–7
Week 1 Review + Ek Habit Choose
Jo tune observe kiya — usme se sirf ek sabse badi problem choose karo. Next week sirf uss par focus hoga.
// Week 1 Mein Sirf Itna Karo
  • Subah uthke 5 min bina phone ke baitho (literally sirf baitho, kuch mat socho)
  • Raat ko sone se pehle kal ki teen priority likho (ek note mein, 2 min kaam)
  • Paani bottle bharke rakho — jab dikhegi tab peeyoge (brain hack hai)
WEEK 02 Body Ko Signal Do — "Ab Kuch Badal Raha Hai" 🏃 ▾
HealthHabit
8
Pehli Walk — 20 Minutes
Aaj sirf 20 min walk karo — ghar ke bahar. Phone mat le jaana. Sirf chalo aur observe karo.
9–10
Morning Routine — 15 Min Version
Uthna → 5 min silence → paani → 10 push-ups ya 10 squats. Bas. Itna hi. Gym nahi.
11–12
Khaana — Ek Change
Koi diet nahi. Sirf ek rule: subah kuch bhi fried mat khao. Ek fruit, bread, dahi — kuch bhi.
13–14
Raat ki neend — Fix Karo
7-8 ghante sona mandatory hai. Neend mein compromise = brain slow = decisions kharab = money gaya.
🎯
Week 2 ke baad: Tu physically zyada alert feel karega. Yeh feel remember karna.
WEEK 03 Mind Ko Declutter Karo 🧠 ▾
MentalHabit
15
Brain Dump Exercise
Ek page lo aur sab kuch likho jo dimag mein chal raha hai — worries, plans, random thoughts. Judge mat karo.
16–17
Negative People / Content Filter
Toxic ya useless content unfollow/mute karo. Sirf creators follow karo jo actually kuch sikhate hain. Tera feed = tera mind.
18–19
5-Minute Journal Start
Raat ko sirf: 1) Aaj kya accha hua 2) Kya aur better kar sakta tha 3) Kal ka ek goal. 5 min max.
20–21
Comparison Band Karo
"Woh already successful hai" — yeh thought aayi toh immediately apne Day 1 se aaj tak ki progress likho. Comparison khud se hai.
WEEK 04 Clinic Ka Kaam Shuru — First Output 🏥 ▾
MoneySkill
22
Clinic Instagram Audit
Current followers, engagement rate, last 10 posts ka performance note karo. Competitors ke 3 clinic accounts analyse karo.
23–24
Pehla Content Batch Create
5 clinic posts design karo Canva mein — 3 educational, 1 testimonial, 1 promotional. Schedule karo Buffer/Meta Planner se.
25–26
GMB Listing Optimize Karo
Google My Business claim karo (if not done). Photos upload, description write, services add karo. Reviews ke liye plan banao.
27–30
Phase 1 Review + Phase 2 Plan
30 din ka honest review likho. Kya achieve kiya? Kya miss kiya? Phase 2 ke targets set karo — realistic but aggressive.
🏆
Phase 1 Complete: Tu ab ek alag insaan hai. Habits bani hain, mind clear hai, clinic ka kaam shuru ho gaya hai. Phase 2 ke liye ready hai.

"Your personal brand is what people say about you when you're not in the room." — Jeff Bezos
